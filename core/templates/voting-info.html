The Voting Process

Secure, Transparent, and Easy-to-Use Voting System

1. Sign Up

Create an account to start organizing your elections. It's quick and easy!

2. Create an Election

Enter the number of ballots, choose blockchain or database storage, and set up your election.

3. Configure Ballots

Specify the title, voting type (Ranked Choice, First Past the Post, or Yes/No), and add candidates.

4. Upload Voter List

Upload a CSV file containing the names and emails of all voters to ensure secure participation.

5. Start the Election

Start the election and automatically send unique voting links to all registered voters.

6. Cast Your Vote

Voters click their unique links, access their ballots, and cast their votes securely.

7. End the Election

End the election to finalize voting. The system counts the votes and displays the results.
